# Reflection: Mission TCS

### What surprised you?
I was surprised by how much emphasis is placed on "Acceptance Criteria" in Agile. Previously, I thought a user story was just a basic requirement, but writing detailed criteria (the "Given/When/Then" structure) is actually what prevents scope creep and ensures the developer and the client are on the same page. It turns a vague idea into a measurable task.

### What would you ask Claude (AI) next?
I would ask for a breakdown of how to handle "Story Points" for these stories. Specifically: "How do we estimate the complexity of the 'SSO Integration' story compared to the 'Dashboard UI' story to ensure our sprint velocity remains stable?"
